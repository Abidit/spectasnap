# SpectaSnap — 60-Second Demo Script

**Target:** Optical store owner, in-person meeting with iPad

---

## 0–10s: Open the App

Open `/trydemo` on the iPad. The premium dark idle screen loads with gold corner marks and "Try On Your Perfect Pair" heading.

**Say:** "This is SpectaSnap. Your customers use it to try every frame you sell — live on their face."

## 10–15s: Start Camera

Tap **Enable Camera**. Face is detected instantly — "AR Live" badge appears.

**Say:** "No app to download. Works right in the browser."

## 15–30s: Browse Frames

Scroll the bottom carousel. Tap 3 different frames — each switches instantly on the face.

**Say:** "50 frame styles. They switch in real time. Watch."

Tap a color swatch to change the frame color.

**Say:** "Every frame, every colour. Customers see exactly what they're buying."

## 30–40s: Staff Panel

Point to the right sidebar showing frame details, face shape tag, and staff note.

**Say:** "Your staff sees the recommendation too — which frame fits which face shape. Helps them close the sale."

## 40–50s: Save & Share

Tap **Save Look** to download a snapshot with SpectaSnap watermark.

Tap **Share** to open WhatsApp sharing.

**Say:** "Customer saves their look, shares it with family on WhatsApp. Free marketing for your store."

## 50–60s: Close

**Say:** "Takes 5 minutes to set up. First 30 days free. After that, flat ₹2,999 per month. Want to try it right now?"

Hand them the iPad.

---

## Tips

- Always have good lighting
- Hold iPad at eye level
- Let them try it themselves — the "wow" moment sells it
- If they ask about cost: "Free for 30 days. Then ₹2,999/month. Cancel anytime."
