# SpectaSnap — WhatsApp Message Templates

> **Usage notes:**
> - Replace `{{STORE_NAME}}` with the store's trading name
> - Replace `{{CUSTOMER_NAME}}` / `{{OWNER_NAME}}` with the person's first name
> - Replace `{{YOUR_NAME}}` with your own name
> - Replace `{{TRIAL_END_DATE}}` with the actual expiry date (e.g. "28 March")
> - Keep messages as-is — they are tuned for WhatsApp's character display and read receipt timing
> - Send between 10am–6pm local time for best open rates

---

**Use case:** First contact with an optical store owner you have never spoken to. Use after finding them via Google Maps, Instagram, or a referral. Send to a WhatsApp Business number if available.

You can also try it yourself here:
